Welcome!

Hi! My name is Austin Phan. I'm a high school student located in New Hampshire, who loves photography and all things tech. I spend time building computers, tinkering with them, as well as taking photos for my friends and family. I love shooting landscapes, but also enjoy taking portraits. My favorite locations that I've visited include Melbourne, Australia, and Utah, USA. I've previously worked as a freelance photographer for my high school, taking photos of sports events, concerts, and big events.
